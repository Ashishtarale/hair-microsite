Male Pattern Hair Loss

“Receding hairline? Bald spots creeping in? You’re not alone, 50% of Indian men experience pattern baldness before turning 25. Take control with targeted treatments that effectively manage hair loss & help regain confidence. ”

Female Pattern Hair Loss

“Thinning at the crown? Widening part lines? You’re not alone—30% of Indian women face androgenetic alopecia due to ageing, hormonal imbalances, and other factors. Restore fuller, healthier hair with timely management.”

Hair thinning

“When your hair appears to gradually lose density, leaving your scalp more visible, due to thinning of the hair shaft it’s time to boost those strands. Explore hair thinning treatments to restore thickness and volume!”

Hormonal Hair Loss

“Hormonal imbalances caused by conditions like PCOS, thyroid & menopause can disrupt hair’s growth cycle, leading to thinning, shedding & hair loss. Take control with tailored hair loss treatments for healthy regrowth.”
